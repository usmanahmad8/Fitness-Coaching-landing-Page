About

Coach Aaron

Since 2016, Aaron Morrison has been working as a trainer and coach, spending thousands of hours with a variety of clients. From youth to seniors, exercise novices to professional athletes, Coach Aaron has the knowledge, tools, and skills to help you reach your goals. He earned his Diploma of Personal Training from the National Personal Training Institute in Orlando, FL in 2016. From there, he went on to receive multiple certifications from the National Academy of Sports Medicine in 2018, including Certified Personal Trainer, Corrective Exercise Specialist, MMA Conditioning Specialist, and Fitness Nutrition Specialist. During this time, he also attended a 12-week internship at Victory MMA & Hustle Hard Boxing, in Colorado Springs, learning how to really apply his skills to training boxers and MMA fighters. Coach Aaron has worked as a Master Trainer at Crunch Fitness in Colorado Springs, and LA Fitness in Palm Bay, FL.
